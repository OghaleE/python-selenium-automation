How to cook Jollof Rice

Ingredients:

  • 5 medium sized Roma tomatoes, roughly chopped red bell pepper
  • 1 medium sized onion, roughly chopped, set aside
  • 2 scotch bonnet peppers (habanero peppers as they are sometimes called)
  • /4 cup of groundnut oil
  • 3 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 cups of parboiled rice
  • 2 1/2 cups of chicken stock
  • 1 tsp salt to taste
  • 1/2 tsp curry powder

Instructions:

  1. Chop tomatoes, onions, bell pepper, ginger, garlic, scotch bonnet pepper and puree in a blender/food processor.
  2. Cook the pureed mixture in a pot with hot cooking oil over medium-high heat.
  3. Stir together and place a lid over it, reduce heat to low, and simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. Stir in the spices and seasonings (Paprika, thyme, nutmeg, bay leaf, and salt), then add the rice and vegetable or meat stock and stir together. Place a piece of aluminium foil into the pot, covering the entire surface of the rice, then place a lid over the pot.
  5. Cook for 20 minutes on low heat (stirring occasionally). Remove the lid, bin the aluminium foil and give the rice a gentle stir, then return the lid and coo right>l for another 5 minutes..
